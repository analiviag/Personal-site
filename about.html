A Bit About Me

Hello! I'm Analivia, a web developer who likes to make aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly websites for busy people, specifically health care professionals, who otherwise wouldn't have the time or skills for that. When I'm not coding you will find me doing all things mom, dreaming about when my next big trip will be, or just really enjoying the comfort of my space. I am also a dual citizen brazilian-canadian, so we can communicate in English or Portuguese.
